This Privacy Policy describes how this website ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Prostate Health Assessment quiz and access related content. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Summary: We collect minimal information needed to operate the quiz and show you relevant content. We do not sell your personal data. We may earn commissions from products you purchase after clicking links on this site.

01 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our site:

  • Quiz responses: Answers you provide during the health assessment (age range, symptom descriptions, lifestyle habits). These are not linked to your identity unless you voluntarily provide contact details.
  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time on site, referring URL, and click behavior — collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookies and tracking pixels: Used to understand traffic sources (including paid ad platforms such as Google and Facebook) and to optimize the quiz experience.
  • Voluntary contact information: If any opt-in form is present on the site and you submit your name or email, that information is collected and stored.

02 How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To display your personalized quiz results and recommendations
  • To analyze and improve the performance and accuracy of our assessment
  • To measure traffic from paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.)
  • To send marketing communications if you have opted in to receive them
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect against fraudulent or abusive use of the site

We do not use your quiz responses to make automated decisions that have legal or significant effects on you. The quiz results are informational only — see our Medical Disclaimer.

03 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the site. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the quiz to function correctly (e.g., session state).

  • Advertising pixels: Facebook Pixel, Google Tag Manager, or similar tools may be present to track conversions from paid ad campaigns and enable retargeting. These pixels may collect your IP address, browser information, and actions taken on the site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may affect the functionality of the quiz.
